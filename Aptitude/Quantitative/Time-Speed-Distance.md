### Example 4: Boats & Streams
**Q**: A boat can travel 20 km downstream in 2 hours and 12 km upstream in 3 hours. Find the speed of boat in still water and speed of stream.

**Solution**:
- Downstream speed = 20/2 = 10 km/hr
- Upstream speed = 12/3 = 4 km/hr
- Speed in still water = (10 + 4)/2 = 7 km/hr
- Speed of stream = (10 - 4)/2 = 3 km/hr

### Example 5: Meeting Point
**Q**: Two persons A and B start from opposite ends of a 100 km road. A travels at 6 km/hr and B at 4 km/hr. When will they meet?

**Solution**:
- Relative speed = 6 + 4 = 10 km/hr (opposite direction)
- Time to meet = 100/10 = 10 hours
- Meeting point from A's end = 6 Ã— 10 = 60 km

### Example 6: Circular Track
**Q**: Two cyclists start from same point on a circular track of 400m. A travels at 8 m/s and B at 6 m/s in same direction. When will A lap B for the first time?

**Solution**:
- Relative speed = 8 - 6 = 2 m/s
- Time for A to gain one full lap = 400/2 = 200 seconds
